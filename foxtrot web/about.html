We are an innovative platform that aims to give the parent the freedom to choose their caregiver according to their requirements and availability. We guarantee a careful and safe selection of our employees (caregivers) working exclusively with integral people who go through a careful application process and that offers the greatest possible protection to our children. For this reason, our caregivers are able to provide the best child care, in the most conscious, stimulating, caring and responsible way possible. Managed by an efficient team, Foxtrot arose out of a love for education and childcare.
